Your Questions, Answered

Everything you need to know

1

What are you known for?

+

CHARLY'S is known for freshly made churros and waffles. People also love our specialty coffee, matcha lattes, and premium ice cream. Our churros are prepared fresh throughout the day and served with signature dips.

2

Do you serve coffee, matcha, and specialty drinks?

+

Yes, CHARLY'S offers a variety of signature iced coffee and matcha drinks. Whether you're looking for a morning coffee on Lincoln Road or an afternoon matcha latte in Miami Beach, our drink menu is designed to pair perfectly with our fresh desserts.

3

What areas do you serve?

+

CHARLY'S flagship is located on Lincoln Road in Miami Beach, Florida. We serve locals, tourists, families, students, and visitors looking for fresh churros, coffee, matcha, waffles, and ice cream. New locations coming soon in Hollywood Beach and FIU.
